Get to know us

High Volume is a website designed to better your music experience. The idea behind it is to make people interact through songs. You can look for playlists and songs or let others advise you on what to listen to; comment on your favourite artists and tune in to see global top charts. You can also create and join Volumecommunity with people around the world to listen to songs simultaneously and look at your community's top charts of the week. Don't wait any longer, get started on High Volume
